Tao Huang - 黄涛

at Lamma Island in 2023

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai AI Lab and Shanghai Jiao Tong University, supervised by Dahua Lin, Jingbo Wang, and Jiangmiao Pang. I obtained my M.Phil. degree at The Chinese University of Hong Kong and B.Eng. degree at ShanghaiTech. I have spent a wonderful time in Tencent Robotics X and Shanghai Qi Zhi Institute. I am also closely working with Lei Han at Noitom Robotics.

My current research focuses on humanoid robot control. I am open to any academic discussion or collaborations. Welcome to drop me an e-mail at taou.cs13@gmail.com or my wechat: h756058918.
